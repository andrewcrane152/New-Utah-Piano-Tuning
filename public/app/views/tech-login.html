Join Us!

It's easy as   1 . . .   2 . . .   3 !

Step #1

Submit your registration

What we need...

  • Regestering provides basic professional information about the area you service and the type of services you offer.
  • We then play match maker and notify you via text or email of new tuning requests that align with what you offer.

Step #2

Login to view leads

What you will find...

  • Once logged in you will be redirected to an easy to use dashboard that contains all the available leads in our database.
  • Each lead will display all the critical information you need to decide whether the lead you are looking at is right for you.

Step #3

Claim your leads

What you will get...

  • Claiming a lead means that no other technicians will have access to it for the next 10 minutes, giving you time to purchase it.
  • After successfully purchasing a claimed lead you will receive an email containing the contact information and exact address for the lead.
